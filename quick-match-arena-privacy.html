Privacy Policy – Quick Match Arena

Last updated: 2025-12-25

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only:

  • With Apple for purchase processing and restoration

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Your Choices

  • You can uninstall the App at any time.
  • You can manage purchases and subscriptions in your Apple ID settings.
  • You may contact us to request deletion of support messages you sent us.

Changes to This Policy
