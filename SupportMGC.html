About the App

Modern Glow Calculator is an offline calculator with customizable themes and optional In-App Purchases.

No data is collected, and all purchases are securely handled through Apple.

Purchase Problems?

If your custom theme does not unlock after purchase:

  • Tap "Restore Purchases" inside the app
  • Restart the app
  • Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID
